Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Data Controller

This application is a standalone multiplayer board game ("FOCUS") that runs on a self-hosted server. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

2. Data We Process

  • Connection metadata: When you connect, your IP address is temporarily processed by the Node.js server to establish and maintain the Socket.io WebSocket connection. This data is held in memory only while your session is active and is never written to disk, logged to file, or stored in a database.
  • Game state: Your moves (board coordinates only) are transmitted to the opponent in real time. Game state exists only in server memory and is permanently deleted when the last player disconnects from the room.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • No cookies, localStorage, or any form of client-side storage
  • No user accounts, emails, passwords, or authentication
  • No analytics, tracking scripts, or telemetry
  • No advertising or marketing data
  • No third-party embedded content or CDNs

4. Fonts

All fonts are self-hosted on this server. No requests are made to Google Fonts or any external service.

5. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

Processing of IP addresses is based on legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) — necessary for the technical operation of a real-time multiplayer game. No personal data is retained after the connection ends.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–21)

Since we do not store any personal data, there is no data to access, rectify, erase, or port. If you believe your rights have been infringed, you may contact us. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

7. Data Retention

All game state and connection metadata are held exclusively in volatile server memory. They are permanently destroyed when the game room is closed (all players disconnected). No backups, logs, or persistent storage exist.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact the server administrator at the address where this application is hosted.
